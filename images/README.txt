stegg.dev Starter Template — Images Folder
===========================================

Rename your photos to match these filenames exactly,
then drag them into this folder. That's it.

REQUIRED FILENAMES:
-------------------
hero.jpg        → Your hero section photo (right side of hero)
about.jpg       → Your about section photo
og-image.jpg    → Social share preview (1200 x 630px) — create free at canva.com

PORTFOLIO PHOTOS (optional — use as many as you have):
-------------------------------------------------------
project-1.jpg
project-2.jpg
project-3.jpg
project-4.jpg
project-5.jpg
project-6.jpg

LOGO (optional):
----------------
logo.png        → Transparent background recommended
                  After adding, update content.js: logo: "images/logo.png"

TIPS:
-----
- JPG works for photos, PNG works for logos/graphics
- No photo yet? Leave it out — placeholder boxes show instead
- Don't have 6 portfolio photos? Just add what you have
- All filenames must be lowercase with no spaces
